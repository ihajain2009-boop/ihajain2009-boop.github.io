About InTeen

Bridging the gap between ambitious students and forward-thinking companies

Our Mission

InTeen was born from our own experiences as high school students struggling to find meaningful opportunities. We saw firsthand how difficult it was for students like us to connect with companies that valued our potential and fresh perspectives.

We're on a mission to transform how students and companies connect. By creating a platform built by students for students, we understand the unique needs of both sides - students seeking real-world experience and companies looking for emerging talent.

Our vision is a world where every student has access to quality internship experiences that shape their future, and every company can easily discover and nurture the talent that will drive their innovation forward.

What We Do

For Students

We provide a curated platform where students from 9th grade to 2nd year college can discover internship opportunities that match their skills, interests, and academic background. Our intelligent matching system connects you with companies that value your potential and are committed to providing meaningful learning experiences.

For Companies

We help companies tap into the incredible potential of student talent. Our platform makes it easy to find motivated interns who bring fresh perspectives, digital native skills, and enthusiasm to your organization. We handle the matching process so you can focus on mentorship and growth.

Our Approach

Unlike traditional internship platforms, InTeen is built by students who understand what students need. We specialize in connecting companies with school and early college students who bring unique advantages – adaptability, fresh perspectives, and digital fluency.

Our Story

As high school students ourselves, we experienced firsthand the frustration of wanting to gain real-world experience but finding few opportunities that took us seriously. The traditional internship world seemed designed for college graduates, leaving ambitious high school students like us on the sidelines.

InTeen was born from late-night conversations, countless cups of coffee, and a shared belief that students deserve better. We started building this platform in our dorm rooms, fueled by the vision of creating opportunities for students who, like us, were eager to learn, grow, and make an impact.

What began as a school project quickly evolved into something much bigger. We realized we weren't alone in our struggle, and that countless other students faced the same barriers. Today, InTeen is more than just a platform - it's a movement to empower the next generation of innovators, creators, and leaders.

We're proof that you don't need to wait until college to start building your future. With passion, determination, and the right opportunities, anything is possible.
