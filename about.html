About InTeen

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a supportive and meaningful space for high school students to explore opportunities, work with startups, and discover their interests in a real-world setting.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make internships accessible to every teenager—helping them grow, gain confidence, and understand the professional world long before they step into it.

Note from the Founders

As high school students ourselves, we deeply relate to the confusion, pressure, and curiosity that define these years. We wanted to build something that makes the journey easier — a platform where teens feel guided, understood, and empowered.

InTeen is our step toward giving students a chance to grow in a safe, supportive, and exciting environment. We hope you find value, opportunities, and a community here.
