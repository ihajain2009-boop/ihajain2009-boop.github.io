About Us

InTeen is a non-profit organisation dedicated to providing meaningful internship opportunities to high school students across India. In a country where academic achievement often dominates focus, real-world exposure can be rare. We bridge that gap by connecting motivated teens with companies and startups ready to mentor and include them — creating pathways for practical learning, confidence, and career clarity.

Our Vision

We envision an India where every teenager — regardless of background — can access real-world learning experiences that shape their future. InTeen aims to normalize early internships, spark curiosity, and empower students to explore career paths with confidence.

Our Mission

InTeen serves as a bridge between India’s young minds and the startup ecosystem. By providing early exposure to workplaces, we aim to help teenagers discover their interests, build real skills, and grow into future-ready individuals.

Note From the Founders

When we started InTeen, we knew exactly what it felt like to be high-school students searching for real opportunities. As teens ourselves, we understand the frustration of wanting to learn, grow, and gain experience — but having nowhere to start. This platform was created so students like us could finally have a place to explore careers, take initiative, and discover what they’re truly capable of.

We hope InTeen becomes the first step in your journey — the one that opens doors, builds confidence, and shapes your future in ways that classrooms alone cannot.

~ Ridima Gupta & Iha Jain
Co-Founders, InTeen
