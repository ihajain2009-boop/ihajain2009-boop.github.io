About InTeen

Bridging the gap between ambitious students and forward-thinking companies

Founded by Students, For Students

Created by high school students who understand the challenges of finding meaningful opportunities

Our Mission

InTeen was born from our own experiences as high school students - the frustration of seeing talented peers struggle to find internship opportunities while companies missed out on the incredible potential we know students possess.

We're on a mission to transform how students and companies connect. As students ourselves, we understand what our generation needs - real opportunities to learn, grow, and make an impact. We're building bridges that lead to mutual growth and success.

Our vision is a world where every student has access to quality internship experiences that shape their future, and every company can easily discover and nurture the talent that will drive their innovation forward.

What We Do

For Students

We provide a platform built by students, for students. From 9th grade to 2nd year college, we help you discover internship opportunities that actually match your skills and interests. No more generic applications - we connect you with companies that value your unique perspective.

For Companies

We help companies tap into the incredible potential of student talent. As digital natives with fresh perspectives, we bring energy and innovation to your organization. We make it easy to find motivated interns who can grow with your company.

Our Approach

Unlike traditional internship platforms, InTeen is built by students who understand what both sides really need. We specialize in connecting companies with school and early college students because we believe in the power of young minds to drive real change.

From The Founders

As high school students navigating our own academic journeys, we kept hitting the same wall - the incredible gap between the talent we saw in our peers and the opportunities available to us. We watched brilliant friends with amazing ideas struggle to find platforms that took them seriously.

InTeen started as a conversation between two friends who believed we could do better. We knew that students our age have so much to offer - fresh perspectives, digital fluency, and passion that can't be taught. We also knew that companies were missing out on this incredible resource.

So we decided to build the solution ourselves. A platform where students aren't just applicants, but valued partners in innovation. Where companies can discover the next generation of thinkers, creators, and leaders.

This is more than just a website to us - it's our commitment to creating the opportunities we wish we'd had. We're building this for every student who's ever been told "you're too young" or "wait your turn." Your time is now.

Join us in creating a future where age is never a barrier to making an impact.

~ Ridima Gupta & Iha Jain
Co-Founders, InTeen
