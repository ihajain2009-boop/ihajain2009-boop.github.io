About Us

InTeen was created with one purpose in mind — to provide high-schoolers with real, practical exposure to the startup world. We wanted to build a bridge connecting ambitious teens with early-stage founders who value enthusiasm, creativity, and a fresh perspective.

Our Vision

To help young students explore, grow, and express themselves through meaningful, real-world opportunities — long before they step into college or the workforce.

Our Mission

We aim to create an inclusive and accessible space where teens can discover internships, build confidence, collaborate with founders, and gain hands-on experience that sparks curiosity and long-term growth.

Note from the Founders

As high school students ourselves, we know how confusing—and sometimes intimidating—it can feel to start exploring opportunities beyond academics. InTeen was born from the same excitement and uncertainty we felt. We wanted to make the journey easier, warmer, and more accessible for students like us. Our hope is simple: that InTeen becomes the platform we wish we had when we first began searching for meaningful experiences.

~ Ridima Gupta & Iha Jain, Co-Founders, InTeen
