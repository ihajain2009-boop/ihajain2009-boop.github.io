Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account as a student?
Simply click on the "Sign Up" button and select "I'm a Student." Fill in your details including your school information, and you'll be ready to start exploring internship opportunities in minutes!
What types of companies post internships on InTeen?
We work with a diverse range of companies from startups to established organizations across various industries including technology, marketing, design, research, and more. All our partner companies are vetted to ensure quality opportunities.
Is there any cost for students to use InTeen?
No, InTeen is completely free for students! We believe in making internship opportunities accessible to everyone, so there are no hidden fees or subscription costs for students.
How long does it take to hear back after applying?
Most companies respond within 1-2 weeks of application. You can track your application status in your dashboard, and we'll notify you as soon as there's an update from the company.
Can international students apply for internships?
Yes! Many of our internships are remote and open to international students. Some location-specific opportunities may have restrictions, which will be clearly mentioned in the internship description.
